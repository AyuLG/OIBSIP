# 🎨 Tribute Page - Dr. Jordan B. Peterson

## 📌 Overview

A visually engaging tribute page dedicated to **Dr. Jordan B. Peterson** — clinical psychologist, bestselling author, and public intellectual. This is **Task 2** of my Web Development Level 2 internship at **Oasis Infobyte**.

The page highlights his journey, achievements, major works, and philosophy through a thoughtfully designed, psychology-themed layout.
